About

Kolkata also known as Calcutta is the capital of the Indian state of West Bengal located on the Eastern Bank of the Hooghly River, the city is approximately 80 km west of the border with Bangladesh. It is the third-most populous metropolitan area in India. Kolkata has been called the "City of Furious, Creative Energy" as well as the "cultural” capital of India as it is known for its literary, artistic and revolutionary heritage thoughts. You’ll get to know more about this wonderful city if you browse further more in this webpage.

So, what are you waiting for. Come.....visit & explore Kolkata, the most cultural and ethically diverse, city of India. Where rustic village life co-exists besides modern metropolitan high rises and bullock carts running parallel to Audis, BMW, Mercedes.
